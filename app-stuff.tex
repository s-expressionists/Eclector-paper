\section{Appendices}

\subsection{A deeply nested expression}
\label{app-nesting}

When given the expression below for evaluation, \sbcl{} fails with a
stack overflow after almost a minute (on a reasonably recent machine)
of processing time, whereas \eclector{} succeeds in a fraction of a
second.

\begin{verbatim}
(let ((string (with-output-to-string (stream)
                (format stream "(#1=1~%")
                (loop :for i :from 2  :below 10000
                      :do (format stream
                                  " #~D=~V,,,'(<(~>#~D#~V,,,')<~>~%"
                                  i 35 (1- i) 35))
                (format stream ") ~%"))))
  (read-from-string string))
\end{verbatim}
